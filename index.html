Cislunar Mining

Resource extraction in the Earth-Moon system — mining lunar regolith, volatiles, metals, and helium-3 from the surface and near-surface to supply water, oxygen, propellant, construction materials, and rare elements for cislunar operations and beyond.

Cislunar mining powers in-situ resource utilization (ISRU), reduces dependency on Earth resupply, and unlocks the economic potential of the lunar frontier under Artemis and commercial frameworks.
